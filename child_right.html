General Trends (Female, Minority, Veterans)

Trend scan
Overall headcount trend (Female, Minority, Veterans) — DOC (2022–2025).

What’s happening in DOC overall?

  • Female headcount remains relatively stable, with a modest upward trend over time.
  • Minority headcount shows a steady upward trajectory between 2022 and 2025.
  • Veteran headcount declines year over year, and the share of the workforce also drops.

Next, we deep dive into Minority, Female, and Veterans to understand what is driving these patterns.

Veteran headcount (bars) and % of total workforce (line), 2022–2025.

What we observe (2022–2025)

The combined chart shows a consistent decline in both veteran headcount and the veteran share of the workforce.

Key observations:
  • Veteran headcount declines year over year.
  • Veteran % of workforce also drops, indicating the decline is not only due to workforce growth.
Female representation (Male vs Female) by year (2022–2025).

What we observe (2022–2025)

The donut charts show DOC remains male-majority, but female representation trends slightly upward.

Key observations:
  • Female share increases modestly over time and contribute to the 1/4 th population in 2025.
  • The year-to-year change is gradual - suggesting steady progress rather than a one-time jump.

Female representation is increasing in DOC.

Minority workforce by race and year (2022–2025).

1) Minority growth by race (2022–2025)

This chart breaks down minority headcount by race over time and highlights which groups are driving overall minority growth..

What we observe:
  • Hispanic representation is the largest and shows the strongest growth across years.
  • Black representation remains strong and increases steadily over time.
  • Asian representation stays relatively stable with small year-to-year changes.
  • Smaller groups (NH/PI, AI/AN) remain low and largely flat.
  • Two or More Races remain constant.

Next, we check whether this minority growth is broad across the organization or concentrated in specific job categories.

2) Minority representation by EEO Job Category (year-by-year)

What this reveals:
  • Protective Service: Sworn dominates minority representation across all years.
  • Professionals is the next meaningful contributor and trends upward gradually.
  • Most other categories remain comparatively small and show limited change.
