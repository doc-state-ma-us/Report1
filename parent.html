Section 4 - Growth rate comparison: Secretariat vs. Massachusetts

Pace check

This chart shows percent growth (2022–2024) for Secretariat headcount vs. statewide population.

Key comparison (2022 → 2024)

  • Asian: Massachusetts 10.20% vs Secretariat 17.53% ✔ ahead of pace
  • Hispanic: Massachusetts 9.63% vs Secretariat 16.75% ✔ ahead of pace
  • Black: Massachusetts 10.02% vs Secretariat 6.53% ⚠ behind pace
  • Two or More Races: Massachusetts 16.31% vs Secretariat 12.24% ⚠ behind pace

Takeaway: gaps are not only in counts, but in growth pace.

Section 5 - Final interpretation

So what?

Key takeaways

  • Ahead of pace: Asian and Hispanic.
  • Behind pace: Black and Two or More Races.
  • Action: prioritize recruitment and retention for Black and Two or More Races employees to close the growth gap.
